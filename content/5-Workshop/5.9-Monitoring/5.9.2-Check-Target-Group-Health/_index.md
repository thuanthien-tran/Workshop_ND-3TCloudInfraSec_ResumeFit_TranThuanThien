### 5.9.2. Health Status Validation

1. Under **Load Balancing**, click **Target Groups** and choose the associated Target Group.
2. Under the **Targets** tab, examine register details.
3. Confirm the status shows a green `Healthy` tag, proving the web container is up and running.
