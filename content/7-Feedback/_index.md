Below is my honest feedback and reflection after completing the 12-week journey of the **First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ)** program in collaboration with AWS Vietnam:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Learning & Working Environment**  
The learning and community environment at FCAJ is highly dynamic and inspiring. Attending in-person events at the AWS Vietnam office (26th & 36th Floor, Bitexco Financial Tower) allowed me to experience a professional corporate setting, which strongly motivated my career goals.

**2. Support from Mentor & Organizers**  
The mentors (especially Mr. Gia Hung and the guest speakers at the events) are incredibly supportive and knowledgeable. Despite their busy schedules, they dedicated time to clarify technical issues and taught us the mindset to troubleshoot problems systematically rather than just giving away copy-paste answers.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
As a student majoring in **Cyber Security** at HUTECH University, I was initially worried that Cloud/AI bootcamps might not align with my path. However, while building the **AI Resume Matching Platform** project, I was able to apply security principles firsthand by configuring mTLS, VPC Origins, setting up RDS Security Groups, and managing API keys securely using AWS Secrets Manager. This practical approach closely aligned with my major.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
The program provides a well-structured roadmap from basics to advanced levels (from AWS Free Tier, EC2, S3 up to advanced 3-Tier architectures, Terraform IaC, and GenAI Bedrock). I not only consolidated my technical skills but also learned how to write technical blogs, manage personal knowledge (Second Brain), and manage project scopes.

**5. Community Culture & Team Spirit**  
FCAJ has a strong culture of sharing. The Facebook Group is active, where technical posts are reviewed quickly and receive high engagement. The Community Day events give students the opportunity to practice presenting their ideas confidently in front of a large audience.

**6. Program Policies & Support for Members**  
The organizers prepared learning resources carefully, provided lovely souvenirs (swag) at in-person events, and sponsored AWS cloud credits to support students during hands-on lab sessions.

---

### Feedback Questions

*   **What did you find most satisfying during your participation in the program?**  
    It was the networking. I had the chance to meet and connect with leading experts at AWS Vietnam, as well as experienced Platform and DevOps Engineers. Additionally, building a complete 3-Tier Cloud application integrated with AI boosted my confidence in my engineering skills.
*   **What do you think the program should improve for future cohorts?**  
    The volume of knowledge is quite high for a 12-week bootcamp (especially advanced topics like Multi-Agent systems or Terraform IaC in the final weeks). The organizers could allocate more practice time between weeks or offer additional online mini-tutoring sessions to assist students who require extra guidance.
*   **If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they join?**  
    Absolutely. This is one of the few hands-on programs for students that brings high-quality practical knowledge, ensuring that we learn by doing rather than just studying dry theory.

---

### Suggestions & Expectations

*   I hope the FCAJ community will continue hosting periodic Community Days to maintain a continuous learning network for future cohorts.
*   In the future, I look forward to participating in the program as an assistant or mentor to guide the next generation of students based on the experiences I accumulated over the last 12 weeks.
